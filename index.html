If you have never heard of basketball or have ever played it, here are a few notes before you dive deeper into the website. First of all, basektball is a sport played by ten people at a time. There are five players on each team and their goal is to score on the opposing players basketball hoop that is located on each end of the court. In order to score, each team will have to shoot or score the the basketball and make it pass through the opposing team's net. Each basket will be worth two points. However, Shots that are made beyond the 3-point line, a line that circles around the basket, will count as a three points. In order for a player to move with the ball, they will have to "dribble" the ball across the court or pass it to another player on their team. The team that has the most points at the end of the game wins.
